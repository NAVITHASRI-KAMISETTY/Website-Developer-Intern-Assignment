About Us

The Journey Behind Zylentrix was founded on the belief that talent, technology, and transformation are the driving forces of the future. Our journey began with Arun Mohan, an Electronics and Communications Engineer and experienced sales and business development professional who, after a decade of industry experience in India, moved to the UK in 2022 to pursue an MBA at Nottingham Business School. While studying, he realised that many students, job seekers, and businesses struggled to access the right guidance, opportunities, and technological solutions to navigate an increasingly competitive global landscape.
